Mother

My mother is a very interesting woman and is someone who I look up to. Her name is Nicole and she is the Vice President of Finance and Administration at the University of New England. My mom has done a lot of very good things for the school and has effectively turned it around and improved it in every way she possibly could. She has published an article in The Business Officer and has done numerous podcasts for NACUBO (National Association of College and University Business Officers) where she is on the board and is also the vice chair for EACUBO (Eastern Association of college and University Business Officers). So, throughout my entire life my mother has been doing all these really neat things in the business world which I think inspired me to pursue a career in business.
